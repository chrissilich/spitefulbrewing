It all started like this...

Brad Shaffer and Jason Klein decided to start a brewery in 2010 because they tired of drinking garbage beer.

In search of a better version of the elixir of life, they decided to not use their debt-filled and four-years-wasted degrees (five for Jason) to find “real jobs.” At least that’s what Mr. and Mrs. Shaffer said.

And now, those wasted nights “testing” different brews didn’t go to waste. What started as a small brewery in Chicago has expanded into select stores across the country. Take that, corporate America! We hope to share the Spiteful lifestyle with you.

Drink our beer.

Minnie The Moocher Double IPA

It’s the same story every time you see her: she shows up carrying nothing but a fierce appetite for food and booze and a remarkable way of panhandling her way into all of the party’s festivities. We’ve crammed twice the Mosaic hops into this double IPA to help relieve the pain felt by all of those in her path. We also suggest camouflaging this beer in the back of the fridge. A head of lettuce makes a nice deterrent.

Burning Bridges Brown Ale

With a healthy serving of hops (Horizon, Faconer’s Flight 7C, and Cascade), this American Brown Ale keeps the bitterness alive from any past disagreements, or give you the testicular fortitude to finally burn that bridge. Balanced with amber and chocolate malt, this ale has a smooth finish that will relieve your conscious of any feelings of guilt you may have of leaving those people in the past.

Malevolence Russian Imperial Stout

A collaboration brew made with the commie bastards Carlos (Coraza) and Adrian (Dryhop). This stout is a unique blend of specialty malts and a heavy addition of Piloncillo sugar we found in a bodega. A truly delicious beer, but beware – the 10.5% ABV can make you spout about crazy ideologies and things you may regret saying the next day, creating high levels of tension that may last, I don’t know, 44 years.

Jackass O’Lantern Pumpkin Ale

We like to think we majored in Harmless Jackassery, with a minor in Unapologetic Authenticity. In the fall, we shed our inhibitions like trees shed leaves and our bad behavior sprouts like a pumpkin seed. To honor those who share in our autumn debauchery, we bring you a $%#& ‘em all beer for your $%#& ‘em all attitude.

G.F.Y. Stout

Hey, go fuck yourself. Let me enjoy my beer. We all want to say it when someone is annoying us when all we want to do is enjoy this roasted barley stout. G.F.Y. lets you say that in this internet-friendly, politically correct world all by slamming the label in the direction of your annoyance. Then slam back the rest of this smooth and chocolaty stout. If they persist, lose the acronym and just tell ‘em to go fuck themselves.

Debbie Downer Dunkelweizen

Whether you want to constantly complain about your crazy significant other or your horrible day at work (so we hear – we work at a frickin’ brewery!), this German wheat beer can uplift your spirits. With a wonderful combination of Munich malt, Hallertauer Hersbrucker hops, and German yeast, complain, forget, or fall into a deeper depression (drink responsibly, kids).

Jingle Balls Winter Ale

Hey, come taste our delicious Balls! This spiced winter ale is brewed with freshly juiced ginger, fresh ground nutmeg, and fresh cinnamon sticks. Yup, really fresh Balls. This spicy Balls concoction will make you all warm inside to get you through those long winter nights. Best drunk in pairs.

Chatty Cathy IPA

You don’t care about her belt buckle opinion on politics and you certainly don’t want to hear about her boyfriend’s erectile dysfunction at the ripe age of 29. You try to walk away, but she just follows. “I’m oversharing again, aren’t I?” she says before beginning another monologue.
